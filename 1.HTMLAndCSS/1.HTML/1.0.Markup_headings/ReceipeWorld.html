Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world had even known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
  3. Pour into a greased baking dish
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
  5. Let cool and enjoy!
